About Us

Welcome to Toury Travel Agency

Toury Travel Agency was founded in 2025 with a vision to make global travel experiences more accessible, enjoyable, and memorable for travelers worldwide. We are dedicated to connecting people with incredible destinations across the globe and helping them create memories that will last a lifetime.

Our team of experienced travel consultants specializes in crafting personalized journeys that cater to your specific needs and preferences. With our deep knowledge of destinations, local customs, and insider tips, we ensure that your travel experience is both enriching and comfortable.

Our Mission

At Toury, our mission is to facilitate transformative travel experiences by providing exceptional services that prioritize safety, comfort, and authentic adventures. We strive to make global exploration accessible to all travelers, regardless of their background or experience with international travel.

Why Choose Us

• Carefully selected accommodations to suit your budget and preferences
• Local tour guides who provide authentic cultural insights

• Competitive pricing with transparent policies

PRIVACY POLICY

At Toury Travel Agency, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Passport details (for booking international travel)
  • Payment details
  • Travel preferences and history
  • Accommodation requirements
  • Dietary restrictions and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process flight, hotel, and tour bookings
  • Process payments securely
  • Send booking confirmations and travel updates
  • Communicate with airlines, hotels, and tour operators on your behalf
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improve our services and personalize your experience
  • Send relevant travel recommendations (only with your permission)
  • Send marketing emails about special offers (only with your permission)

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure servers to protect your personal information. Only authorized employees have access to your data on a need-to-know basis.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Airlines, hotels, and tour operators (only as needed for your booking)
  • Payment processors (for transaction purposes only)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze traffic patterns. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally possible)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
